# Hot Pot
Based on Mikey Chen's quick hot pot recipe!

## Ingredients
### Broth
- 10 dried chilies
- 6 slices of ginger
- 1 tbsp goji berries
- 10 dried dates
- 4 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 6 cloves of garlic
- 1 tbsp chinese pepper corn
- 6 bay leaves
### Sauce
- 1.5 tbsp Chinese bbq sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame paste
- 1 tsp Chinese vinegar
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Hot oil to taste
